ABC SCHOOL

ABC School, one of the oldest International Schools in the country was founded in 1985. Situated at 232, Baudhaloka Mawatha, Colombo 7. Wycherley boasts of 30 years of academic excellence that has resulted in exemplary achievements of our students, with quality education, on par with global standards. Wycherley is also the First and Only Comprehensive Centre for Cambridge Education from Year 1 to Year 13 in Sri Lanka. The School teaches English not as a foreign language but as a unifying agent that draws together students from a multi-racial, multi-ethnic, multi-religious society. It has the right ambience that unites Sinhala, Tamil, Muslim, Malay, Burgher and even foreign students
